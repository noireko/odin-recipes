Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions. The combination of flavors and textures makes it a favorite among many.F

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and garlic until translucent.
    3. Add ground beef or sausage, cook until browned, then drain excess fat.
    4. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with some salt and pepper.
    6. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    7. Layer noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and more meat sauce.
    8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
    9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
    10. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
