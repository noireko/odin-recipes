Description

A salad is a dish consisting of mixed pieces of food, typically with at least one raw ingredient. It can be served as an appetizer, side dish, or main course.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens.
  2. Chop the cucumber and red onion.
  3. Halve the cherry tomatoes.
  4. Toss all ingredients in a large bowl.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
