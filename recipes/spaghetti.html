Description

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta of Italian origin. It is made from durum wheat semolina and water, and is typically served with a variety of sauces.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Add spaghetti pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the pan and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
